About Agilvax

Agilvax Inc. develops targeted vaccine therapies for infectious diseases and cancer utilizing its proprietary virus-like particle (VLP) platform technology. The company has a pipeline and platform that is based on VLPs derived from RNA bacteriophage that have been engineered to incorporate novel vaccine components. This platform allows the company to rapidly identify ideal VLP candidates to create novel and effective vaccine therapies. This novel VLP platform facilitates both vaccine discovery and implementation through a unique affinity selection process (reverse vaccinology). The Company was founded in 2011 and has headquarters in Albuquerque, NM, USA.
